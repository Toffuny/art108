A Work in Progress


Blog Post #6

My partner and I's game, titled Bunny Haunt, is an in progress 2D top down maze game. The premise of the game is that players are a girl dressed up as a bunny getting lost in a maze and has to find their way to an exit before the ghosts take her soul away. As for the art and music being used in the creation of the game, the all of the art is created by my partner, the designer, while the background music has been taken from ZapSplat. The way to play the game is simply just using the arrow keys to move the character around the maze and the spacebar to shoot projectiles at the ghosts that randomly spawn to kill you. The game still isn't finished yet, but we've already come across a problem where the projectile just briefly shows on top of the character whenever a player presses the space bar and then just disappears.... I (the programmer) still have to fix the problem. Compared to the alpha test though, we shifted the game completely from having players being a circle that shoots at other incoming circles to having the character and enemy art, the maze, and the ability to move with the arrow keys. One thing that has fallen apart since the test was that the shooter aspect no longer works.
